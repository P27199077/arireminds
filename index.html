Setup Guide

Learn how to get the most out of your Ari Reminds widget helper.

Step-by-step Setup

1

Add Tasks

Go to the Task Manager tab and register your tasks for the day. Setting tasks makes the reminder relevant and feeds stats to your progress dashboard.

2

Create Custom Reminders

Navigate to the Reminders Manager tab and click **Create Reminder**. You can configure a gym check-in at 6:00 PM and a running alert at 7:00 AM separately!

3

Upload Isolated Videos

Inside the edit form of any reminder profile, you can upload specific clips (MP4/WebM) representing the mascot actions. If none are provided, the app plays *Ari*, a hand-designed CSS-animated fallback character, completely free of charge!

4

Start the Application Natively

Ensure that you keep the Ari Reminds desktop dashboard open in the background of your laptop. The main clock monitor will automatically check schedules and trigger mascot overlays at the exact configured times.

Quick Test Helper: You can click the **Test Play (play button)** directly on any reminder profile card inside the Reminders Manager tab to bypass scheduled clocks and immediately run its specific assets sequence!
